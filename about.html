About Trilogy Pictures

Trilogy Pictures develops and produces prestige film and TV projects, rooted in African stories with global resonance. Our ethos is minimalism: clarity of vision, depth of craft, and cinematic storytelling that transcends borders.

Team

Tobi Bamtefa

Tobi Bamtefa is a Nigerian-British actor with extensive theatre, film, and television credits. On screen, he has appeared in Tin Star (Sky), Feel Good, The Witcher (Netflix), Invasion (AppleTV), and Mayor of Kingstown. His film work includes Lost in London, BAFTA-nominated Responsible Child, Horrible Histories: The Movie, and How to Build a Girl. On stage, Tobi has performed at the National Theatre in Network and Three Sisters, starred in The Last King of Scotland at Sheffield Crucible, and toured internationally with Inua Ellams’ Barbershop Chronicles.

Ladi Emeruwa

Ladi Emeruwa is a London-based Nigerian actor and producer. He played the title role in Shakespeare’s Globe’s landmark world tour of Hamlet, performing in nearly every country worldwide. His screen credits include Berlin Station (Epix), Humans (Channel 4/AMC), Into the Badlands (AMC/Amazon), and Riviera (Sky).

Africa Ukoh

Africa is a screenwriter, playwright, and theatre director whose work blends a distinctly African voice with global appeal. He co-wrote Green White Green and The Lost Okoroshi, both of which premiered at TIFF and screened at Berlinale and BFI London Film Festival. His play 54 Silhouettes has been produced by the BBC, staged internationally, and won Best International Show at the United Solo Theatre Festival in New York.
